Cambugahay Falls

Coco Grove Beach Resort, located on the Siquijor Circumferential Road in San Juan, is a luxurious haven famous for its long 800 meters private white sand beach and immediate access to the lively Tubod Marine Sanctuary. Visitors can select from a variety of cottages and villas designed in a native style, surrounded by lush tropical gardens, with features such as two swimming pools, a swim-up bar, and three unique dining options. Renowned for its outstanding snorkeling and diving right from the beach, the resort mixes a calm, romantic vibe with family-oriented amenities, featuring a kids' club and an on-site spa. Coco Grove offers reliable private transfers using their motor yacht along with well-organized island tours, ensuring a complete and esteemed getaway on Siquijor Island.

Old Enchanted Balete Tree + Fish Spa

The Old Enchanted Balete Tree in Lazi, Siquijor, is a must-visit mystical landmark centered around a massive, 400 years old strangler fig tree that local folklore holds as a dwelling place for spirits and magical beings. A unique, natural spring emerges from its base, filling a man-made pool. This easily accessible roadside attraction seamlessly blends natural awe with local mysticism and lighthearted fun, offering local crafts and refreshments nearby.

Siquijor Butterfly Sanctuary

The Siquijor Butterfly Sanctuary is a delightful, modest conservation initiative situated in the peaceful, hilly region of the island close to Mount Bandilaan. This family-operated project focuses on the promotion of indigenous butterfly species, providing visitors with a serene garden setting to watch the fragile insects throughout their life stages. Managed by enthusiastic locals, the sanctuary functions on a low entrance fee and offers an enlightening and close-up view of Siquijor's initiatives to preserve its natural environment. This peaceful, picturesque location is ideal to visit in the morning when butterflies are highly active, usually paired with other natural sites in the island's central region.
