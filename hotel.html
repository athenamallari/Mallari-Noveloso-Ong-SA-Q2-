Coco Grove Beach Resort (Casa Coco, Siquijor Circumferential Rd, San Juan, Siquijor)

Coco Grove Beach Resort, located on the Siquijor Circumferential Road in San Juan, is a luxurious haven famous for its long 800 meters private white sand beach and immediate access to the lively Tubod Marine Sanctuary. Visitors can select from a variety of cottages and villas designed in a native style, surrounded by lush tropical gardens, with features such as two swimming pools, a swim-up bar, and three unique dining options. Renowned for its outstanding snorkeling and diving right from the beach, the resort mixes a calm, romantic vibe with family-oriented amenities, featuring a kids' club and an on-site spa. Coco Grove offers reliable private transfers using their motor yacht along with well-organized island tours, ensuring a complete and esteemed getaway on Siquijor Island.

The Bruce Resort (Siquijor Circumferential Rd, San Juan, Siquijor)

The Bruce Resort provides a relaxed and welcoming getaway located right at Solangon Beach along the Siquijor Circumferential Road in San Juan. It offers a selection of affordable lodging options, including simple rooms and bungalows. It acts as a practical and cozy place for visitors eager to discover the islands west coast spots.

Tropical Fun-Ta-Sea Rentals (Siquijor Circumferential Rd, San Juan, Siquijor)

Tropical Fun-Ta-Sea Rentals is a friendly,family-oriented resort located on the beach along the Siquijor Circumferential Road in San Juan, Known for its budget-friendly lodgings and studding sea vistas, the resort features various rooms and apartments that serve as a fantastic Starting point for exploring the island. Visitors can enjoy the relaxing vibes and accessible facilities, such as complimentary kayaks and paddleboards, in-houses restaurants and bars offering diverse dishes, and motorbike rentals. This location is perfect for people who are on on a budget but still looking for an enjoyable beach experience with stunning views and a central position close to necessities.
